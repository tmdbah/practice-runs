## Goals

<!-- Goals & requirements -->

Add an explicit lifecycle status to `Session` — `PROPOSED → CONFIRMED / CANCELLED` — driven by manual proposer actions, to address a real incident: a `RENTED_GYM` session was proposed, the group never hit `minPlayers`, and before it firmed up another team booked the slot. There was no way to signal "this fell through" (so nobody accidentally RSVPs into a dead session) or "this is booked for sure" (vs. still gathering RSVPs).

- Schema: new `SessionStatus` enum (`PROPOSED`/`CONFIRMED`/`CANCELLED`) + `Session.status` field, default `PROPOSED`. Not auto-computed from RSVP count — booking is a real-world action, not a threshold crossing.
- Two new dedicated endpoints (`PATCH .../sessions/[sessionId]/confirm` and `.../cancel`), not folded into the existing whole-record-replace `PATCH`.
- Grid header pill goes three-state: solid gold (confirmed), hollow gold outline (proposed), muted (none) — cancelled sessions drop out of the header indicator entirely.
- Cancelled session cards grey out with a "fell through" banner, lose RSVP/Edit ability, and gain a "Propose alternate time here" button that pre-fills a *new* session from the cancelled one's venue/cost/minPlayers — RSVPs on the cancelled card stay as an honest historical record rather than being carried over or deleted.

See full plan: `/Users/tmdbah/.claude/plans/staged-squishing-toucan.md`
